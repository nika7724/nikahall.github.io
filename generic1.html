Unified_Process

I aimed to establish a set of partially ordered steps to systematically achieve my goal of this project. UP is an interactive development process organized into a series of mini projects consisting of the Inception, Elaboration, Construction, and Transition phases. Beginning with the Inception phase, I defined the scope of my project, proceeded to the Elaboration phase for detailed planning and design implementation, focused on coding during the Construction phase, and addressed testing and fine-tuning in the Transition phase. The system evolves incrementally, progressing iteration by iteration. Unlike the traditional waterfall model, the Unified Process allows for revisiting and enhancing existing software.
